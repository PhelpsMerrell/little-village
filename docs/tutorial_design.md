# Tutorial Design — Awakening the Village

## Core Philosophy
- Teach by doing, not reading
- Each step introduces ONE mechanic
- Player must complete an action to progress
- Reinforce color loop:
  - Yellow = growth
  - Blue = control
  - Red = power/survival

---

## Phase 1 — Awakening (Color Shifting)
Bring villagers near the orb to shift colors.

---

## Phase 2 — Growth (Yellow Duplication)
Turn villagers yellow to duplicate and grow population.

---

## Phase 3 — Control (Blue Compression)
Turn villagers blue to merge and reduce population.

---

## Phase 4 — Economy (Stone + Bank)
Yellows gather stone and deposit at the Bank.

---

## Phase 5 — Food Chain (Fish System)
Blues gather fish → bring to Fishing Hut  
Reds eat fish to survive

---

## Phase 6 — Survival Pressure
- Reds eat 1 fish/day
- Starving reds wander toward hut
- Die after 2 days without food

---

## Phase 7 — Expansion (Breaking Doors)
Reds break doors to access new rooms.

---

## Phase 8 — Building System
- Open shop
- Buy house
- Place house

---

## Phase 9 — Housing Villagers
- Put villagers in house
- Release them

---

## Phase 10 — Full Loop
Grow, gather, feed, and expand.

---

## Important Additions

### Why Colors Matter
- Yellow: fast, weak
- Blue: strong, slow
- Red: powerful, must eat

### Decision Tension
Force player to choose:
- convert units OR
- gather resources

### Light Danger
Introduce small threat or enemy

### Room Ownership
Hint at expansion and control

### Time System
Show day cycle

### Selection System
Teach shift-hover multi-select

---

Create a guided tutorial level that introduces:
